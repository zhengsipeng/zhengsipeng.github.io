Research



10/2020-present
Few-shot Action Recognition It is important to build the salient representations for each few-shot action class. Therefore, we exploring spatial/temporal/channel-wise multimodal fusion methods to integrate multi-modalities (image, motion, depth, keypoints etc.) for salient action class prototype learning.

4/2019-4/2020
Video Relation Detection We built a strong relation system with a temporal-aware relational predictor for video relation detection; ranked 2nd in ACM MM 2019 Video Relation Understanding Grand Challenge. We then improved the tracking module of the previous pipeline, which was more efficient in multi-person scenarios and achieved better tracking performance; ranked 4th in ACM MM 2020 Challenge.

6/2018-9.2020
Visual Relation Detection We proposed a multi-level attention method to fuse multiple modalities (i.e. visual appearances, spatial locations, textual semantics) for relation prediction; published in ACM MM 2019. We are also interested in human-object interaction and proposed a graph-based method to effectively utilize human skeletons for fine-grained human- object relation prediction; published in ICME 2020. Our recent work is proposed to build sparse object graphs and a multi-step training strategy to efficiently and effectively reason over contexts for visual relation detection.
